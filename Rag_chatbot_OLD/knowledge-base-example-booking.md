# Motor Service Booking

## How to Book a Service

To book a service for your motor, please [click here](/form/booking-service).

Our service center offers:
- 1st Free Service
- Regular Service
- Repair
- Inspection

## What You'll Need

When booking, please have ready:
- Your motor model (e.g., NVX, Aerox)
- Plate number
- Current mileage
- IC/Passport number
- Contact phone number

## Service Hours

Monday - Friday: 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM
Saturday: 9:00 AM - 2:00 PM
Sunday: Closed

## Confirmation

After submitting your booking, our team will contact you within 24 hours to confirm your appointment slot.

For urgent matters, please call our hotline at 03-1234-5678.
