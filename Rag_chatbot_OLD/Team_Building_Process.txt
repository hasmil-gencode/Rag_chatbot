# Team Building Request Process

## Overview
This document outlines the process for requesting team building activities at GenCode.

## How to Request Team Building

To request a team building activity, employees must follow these steps:

1. **Download the Form**
   - Download the **Team Building Form.doc** from the company portal
   - This form is required for all team building requests

2. **Fill Out the Form**
   Complete all required fields in the Team Building Form.doc:
   - Team/Department Name
   - Proposed Date and Time
   - Number of Participants
   - Budget Estimate
   - Activity Type (Indoor/Outdoor)
   - Preferred Location
   - Special Requirements
   - Manager Approval Signature

3. **Submit the Form**
   - Submit the completed **Team Building Form.doc** to HR Department
   - Email: hr@gencode.com.my
   - Or submit physically to HR office (Level 3, Room 301)

4. **Approval Process**
   - HR will review within 3-5 business days
   - Budget approval required for activities over RM 5,000
   - Final confirmation will be sent via email

## Important Notes

- All team building requests must be submitted at least 2 weeks in advance
- The **Team Building Form.doc** is mandatory - requests without this form will not be processed
- Budget must be approved by department head before submission
- Maximum participants: 50 people per activity

## Contact Information

For questions about team building requests:
- HR Department: hr@gencode.com.my
- Extension: 301
- Office Hours: Monday-Friday, 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Where can I get the Team Building Form?**
A: Download the **Team Building Form.doc** from the Forms section or request from HR.

**Q: How long does approval take?**
A: Typically 3-5 business days after submitting the completed Team Building Form.doc.

**Q: Can I submit the form via email?**
A: Yes, send the completed **Team Building Form.doc** to hr@gencode.com.my.

**Q: What if I need to change the date after approval?**
A: Submit a new **Team Building Form.doc** with updated information.
