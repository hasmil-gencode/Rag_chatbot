## Knowledge Text

```
Q: I want to book a service
A: Sure! Please [click here](/form/booking-service) to book your motor service appointment.

Q: How do I book a motor service?
A: You can book a service by [clicking here](/form/booking-service). We'll need your motor details and preferred appointment time.

Q: Book service
A: To book a service, please [click here](/form/booking-service).

Q: I want to service my motor
A: Great! [Click here](/form/booking-service) to schedule your service appointment.

Q: Schedule maintenance
A: You can schedule your motor maintenance by [clicking here](/form/booking-service).

Q: Service my bike
A: You can book your bike service by [clicking here](/form/booking-service).

Q: Motor repair
A: For motor repair services, please [click here](/form/booking-service) to book an appointment.

Q: What services do you offer?
A: We offer:
- 1st Free Service
- Regular Service
- Repair
- Inspection

To book any of these services, [click here](/form/booking-service).

Q: What are your service hours?
A: Our service center is open:
- Monday to Friday: 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM
- Saturday: 9:00 AM - 2:00 PM
- Sunday: Closed

Q: What do I need to book a service?
A: Please have ready:
- Motor model (e.g., NVX, Aerox)
- Plate number
- Current mileage
- IC/Passport number
- Contact phone number

Q: How long for confirmation?
A: Our team will contact you within 24 hours to confirm your appointment.

Q: What is the booking process?
A: Simply [click here](/form/booking-service), fill in your details, and our team will contact you within 24 hours to confirm.

Q: Can I book now?
A: Yes! [Click here](/form/booking-service) to book your appointment now.

Q: Need to service
A: No problem! [Click here](/form/booking-service) to book your service.

Q: Booking
A: To make a booking, please [click here](/form/booking-service).
```
